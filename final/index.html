Oh the places you'll go!

Welcome to Portal Runner. Recognize that little orb? That's you. And you've got a debt to pay.

Collect as many coins as possible and avoid your enemies... you've got a ton of them.

Luckily, you, the Holy orb, have the ability to travel further and faster than anyone else. How?

If you can reach the portals (arrows), they'll take you to some land far away in space.

If your enemies find you there, worry not! You can use the portals again to return to your planet.

Well ... some planet. Good luck!
